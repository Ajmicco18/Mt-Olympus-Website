Refuges

Spilios Agapitos Hut

The Spilios Agapitos refuge is located in the Balkoni region, in the upper area of the Enipea valley. It is located at an altitude of 2,060 meters and on the main and busiest path of Olympus. The building was originally designed by the first president of E.O.S., Spilios Agapitos and was the only refuge in the mountain for many years. Completed in 1931, could the intial building could only house 25 people, but gradually expanded and improved to its current state which can house 110 people. For information and reservations contact +30 2352081800.

Petrostruga Refuge

The Petrostruga refuge is built on the Petrostruga Pleateau, and has a beautiful view of the coast along the city of Litochoro below. It is located at an altitude of 1,940 meters. This stone shelter has three levels with an independent living area, kitchen and a dormitory. Additionally, a makeshift helipad was built nearby. This building can accommodate up to 74 people and is located at the intersection of multiple routes. For information or reservations contact +30 6971773070

Giosos Apostolidis Refuge

The Gioso Apostolidis Refuge is built between the peaks of Prophet Ilias and Toumba, in front of the Muses Plateau. It is located at an altitude of 2,697 meters, which gives it the highest altitude of any other Greek shelter. It is a two-story stone building with its main feature being a large closed glass window. The building has an organized kitchen and can house 90 people. For information and reservations contact +30 2351082840.

Christos Kakalos Refuge

The Christos Kakalos refuge was built on the southeast edge of the Muses Plateau and is at an altitude of 2,640 meters. It is a vert small two-story building and has an organized kitchen and can accommodate up to 17 people. It is only open from the end of May to the beginning of November. For information, contact +30 2352100009.
