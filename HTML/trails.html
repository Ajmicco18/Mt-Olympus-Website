Trails

Mount Olympus is one of the best locations in all of Greece to hike, with thousands of visitors coming each year. No matter what your experience level, there is a trail for everyone! Mount Olympus has three trails, each with a different level of difficulty from beginner to experienced!

Hermes' Path

  • Difficulty: Easy
  • Max Elevation: 1773 meters
  • Wide, smooth trail suitable for beginners
  • Relaxing and peaceful atmosphere, perfect for leisurely hikes.
  • Shortest trail, not reaching mountain peak

Athena's Ascent

  • Difficulty: Medium
  • Max Elevation: 2253 meters
  • Ideal for intermediate hikers seeking a moderate challenge.
  • Rocky, winding path with a steady incline.
  • Longer trail, finishing at the mountain's plateau

Zeus' Challenge

  • Difficulty: Hard
  • Max Elevation: 2918 meters
  • Perfect for experienced hikers looking for an adventurous challenge.
  • Steep, narrow path with jagged rocks and rugged terrain.
  • Longest, most difficult trail, reaching the mountain's peak
