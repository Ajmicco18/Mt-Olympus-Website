Events

Mythology Festival

5-7 March, 2025 | 10:00-16:00

A cultural event celebrating Greek mythology, featuring storytelling sessions, live performances of mythological plays, and guided tours to myth-inspired landmarks such as the "Throne of Zeus."

For questions visit the official Greek Mythology Festival website.

Olympus Marathon

Saturday, 21 May, 2025 at 12:00

Compete in a marathon that starts at the base of Mount Olympus and runs through some of the scenic trails at lower elevations of the mountain.

Cost: $30 per runner

Interested members should register here!

Stargazing on the Muses Plateau

Every Friday at 22:00 from May to November

A seasonal weekly event where participants gather on the Muses Plateau to enjoy guided stargazing sessions. Expert astronomers discuss the stars and constellations, linking them to Greek mythology.

Contact Hipparchus at star_gazing@olympus.org.

Ancient Dion Archaeological Tour

Tours run daily from 8:00 to 17:00

A guided exploration of the ancient city of Dion, located at the foothills of Mount Olympus. The tour includes visits to temples, theaters, and museums showcasing the region's history.

Cost: $20 per person

Interested members should register here!
